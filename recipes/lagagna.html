Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a deliscious mix of pasta, meat, sauce, and cheese

Originating in Italy, it has become a staple in many countries.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Small layer of sauce
  2. Single layer of pasta in a sheet
  3. Add the meat
  4. Add the cheese
  5. Repeat!
